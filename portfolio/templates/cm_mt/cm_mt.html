    About the project

            Machine translation is a rapidly evolving field in Natural Language Processing (NLP) with applications ranging from improving cross-lingual communication to assisting global business operations. In this project, we address the complex challenge of translating code-mixed language text. Our aim is to develop a robust system that can accurately translate mixed language text (Spanglish and Hinglish) into English, recognizing the nuances of language switching within a sentence. With bilingualism being a significant aspect of our lives, this project has a practical relevance that extends beyond the research realm. Moreover, this work serves as a proof of concept, potentially paving the way for enhanced mixed language translation in various applications. The following are the tasks performed under this project (still under development and tested on a subset of data):

    • Curated dataset for this task. Currently, I am working on adding other languages to our bucket as well.
    • Devised a comprehensive end-to-end module that address the task of machine translation for Hinglish. Following this, I will be adding support for multi-lingual translation as well.
